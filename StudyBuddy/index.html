Introduction

Welcome to StudyBuddy! Here, we help you to find people to study with, aka "studybuddies"

What StudyBuddy aims to do:

      Help you find people to study with by helping you to organise study sessions

How StudyBuddy works:

      1. First you create a session by going to the session page and filling up the create session form
      2. Your newly created session will then be displayed on the sessions page along with all other sessions, all sessions are arranged according to date
      3. You can search for sessions by using the search menu at the above the sessions table
      4. You can choose to delete your session (using the delete checkboxes and pressing the button "Apply") for whatever reason, but please inform the people involved before hand
      5. You can choose to attend somebody else's session (using the attend checkboxes and pressing the button "Apply"), and an email will be sent to the session owner to inform him/her

As StudyBuddy is made with good intentions please do not:

      1. Please do use the study sessions for other purposes
      2. Please do not input any offensive information in the session fields
      3. Please do not input any useless information in the session fields
      4. Please do not select you are going but do not turn up for that particular session. Please inform the people involved before hand
      5. Please create a session but do not turn up for that particular session. Please inform the people involved before hand
